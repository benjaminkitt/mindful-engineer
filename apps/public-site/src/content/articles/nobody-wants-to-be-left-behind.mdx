Every fast team accidentally creates someone who feels late. Usually it is the new hire. Sometimes it is the person who took a week off. Sometimes it is the person who was helping another team while the architecture shifted underneath them.

The fix is rarely to stop moving. The fix is to stop treating legibility as optional. Explain the rename. Keep the migration note. Leave behind the breadcrumbs that would have helped you when you were new.

## Make your speed legible

1. Prefer public explanations over private clarifications.
2. Leave changelog context with the code, not just in chat.
3. Turn local lore into reusable notes before it hardens into hierarchy.

Nobody wants to be the person who joined on the wrong week. Good teams act like they remember that.
